# Support Procedures for Startup Founder Platform

## 1. Initial Consultation Process
**Objective**: Understand founder's needs and current stage

### Steps:
1. Ask about the founder's background and experience
2. Clarify the business idea and industry
3. Determine current stage (idea, MVP, early revenue, etc.)
4. Identify main challenges or questions
5. Set expectations for support and timeline

## 2. Business Guidance
**For founders asking about business models:**

1. Discuss their current business model
2. Ask about target market and customer segments
3. Explore revenue streams and pricing strategy
4. Help create/refine their elevator pitch
5. Provide resources and templates

## 3. Funding Support
**For founders seeking investment:**

1. Assess their readiness for fundraising
2. Help prepare pitch deck outline
3. Guide on investor targeting and outreach
4. Advise on legal/financial documentation
5. Prepare for due diligence process

## 4. Product Development Advice
**For technical/product questions:**

1. Validate the MVP approach
2. Advise on technology stack considerations
3. Help prioritize features
4. Guide on user testing and feedback
5. Discuss product-market fit strategies

## 5. Escalation Protocol
If the question requires specialized expertise:
1. Acknowledge the complexity
2. Ask clarifying questions
3. Provide best available guidance
4. Recommend connecting with specialist mentors
5. Document for future knowledge base expansion

## Response Guidelines
- Be encouraging and supportive
- Provide practical, actionable advice
- Ask clarifying questions when needed
- Share relevant resources and examples
- Follow up with templates and tools when appropriate
